Trainings and Exercises

Training involves structured programs to achieve specific goals, while exercise maintains general health. Together, they enhance strength, endurance, and well-being.

warm-up : Dynamic stretches prepare muscles, improve mobility, and reduce injury risk.

Push-up: strengthen chest, shoulders, triceps, and core with bodyweight resistance.

Plyometric jump: explosive exercise to boost power and agility.

Hamstring: improves flexibility, reduces injury risk, and eases tightness.

Lunge: strengthen legs, glutes, improve balance, and enhance core stability.

Crunch: target abdominal muscles, strengthening core and improving midsection definition.
